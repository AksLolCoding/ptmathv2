What is PTMC?

PeachTree Math Circle (PTMC) is a student-led community consisting of passionate competition math enthusiasts from the greater Atlanta area. Our team members share a common love for problem solving and are commited to helping students of all levels discover the beauty of mathematics.


Our Vision

PTMC was founded with a clear goal: helping more students discover a love for math and enabling them to develop important problem-solving and critical thinking skills. Importantly, PTMC offers entry points for beginners as well as growth opportunities for advanced students.
