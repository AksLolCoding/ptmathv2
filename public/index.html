What is PTMC?

PeachTree Math Circle is a student-run community of learners and mentors focused on deep mathematical thinking. We build spaces where students can explore unfamiliar problems, discuss ideas, and strengthen proof-writing habits.

Guided Exploration

Sessions are designed around rich contest-style and open-ended tasks that reward experimentation, not just memorized techniques.

Welcoming Entry Points

New students can start with foundational circles while advanced participants tackle challenge tracks and national-level prep.
